Palm And Avocado Salad

Details

Ingredient

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 14-inch sprig rosemary , chopped
  • 4 sprigs thyme, chopped
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil.
  2. Mix ground lamb, garlic powder, rosemary, thyme, salt
  3. Place patties on the preheated grill and cook until firm
  4. Mix mayonnaise and mustard together in a small bowl.
